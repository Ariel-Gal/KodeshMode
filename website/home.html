What it does

Everything You Need
for Shabbat

A purpose-built watch app — not just a face — designed for controlled entry, exit, button behavior, and dedicated in-app screens.

Hebrew Display

Hebrew-first UI with embedded bitmap fonts for reliable rendering across all Garmin devices.

Shabbat Mode

Dedicated Shabbat-aware status and display behavior with automatic entry and exit.
