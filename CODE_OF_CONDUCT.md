# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project welcomes respectful, constructive participation.

Examples of positive behavior:

- Being respectful of different levels of experience
- Giving clear, actionable feedback
- Reporting bugs with enough detail to reproduce them
- Being patient with maintainers and contributors

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insults, personal attacks, spam, or intentionally disruptive conduct.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or block users who repeatedly violate these standards.
